Allergy Statement

Before placing your order, please inform your server if anyone in your party has a food allergy.

At Dessert First we are committed to make your experience a sweet one, including those with food allergies and sensitivities. We try to offer something for everyone. We have an allergy sensitive kitchen area to prepare foods for those who require special handling. However, even with processes and procedures in place, we cannot guarantee an allergen free facility. There is always a chance of cross contamination.
